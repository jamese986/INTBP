Laminated Paper Bags


Our luxury Laminated Paper Carriers all come with a Gloss or Matt Lamination as standard.

All sizes are bespoke made to measure to suit your exact requirements. Additional finishes include Foil Blocking, Spot Gloss UV Varnish & Embossing.

  • Various paper types & weights available.
  • All sizes are bespoke made to measure.
  • 1 colour printing up to 4 colour process and beyond. We use the very best machine technology to achieve optimum print quality.
  • Choice of bespoke handle types and colours; from Cotton Cord & P.P through to various Ribbons & Poly Tubing. Metal eyelets also available to match.
  • Our artist will set the bag design for you and will prepare digital proofs for your full approval before any production commences.
  • Pre-production samples also available on request.
