Polythene Carrier Bags


In The Bag Packaging can supply any type of polythene bag to suit your requirement.

From economic low cost budget carriers through to high-grade polythene, we still provide the same level of high quality bespoke printing across the range.

  • Generic handle styles; Patch, Flexiloop, Varigauge, Turn-over-Top, Duffle, Rope &
    Clip-Close.
  • LDPE, HDPE, LLDPE and more.
  • Polythene sealable mailing bags for secure delivery of your goods.
  • Biodegradable & recyclable options available.
  • Fast turnaround and low-cost. A great way to promote your brand.
