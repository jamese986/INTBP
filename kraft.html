Kraft & Eco Bags


If you’re in need of a paper bag which is still great quality, but less extravagant than our luxury Laminated ones, then Kraft Paper is the perfect choice.

These bags have superb strength, are fully recyclable and still come bespoke printed to match your brand. An excellent low-cost walking advertisement.

Alternatively, if you are looking for something still eco-friendly but with even more long-term durability then we supply a full range of bespoke printed Cotton, Canvas, Jute and Non-Woven bags which can simply be used again and again and again…

  • White or Brown Kraft paper. Smooth, ribbed, textured, coated or uncoated paper.
  • Various standard sizes available, or we can make to measure on larger quantity orders.
  • Fully bespoke printed – up to 100% coverage if required, with a varnish to protect the ink.
  • 1 colour printing up to 4 colour process and beyond. Special finishes available on certain types of paper.
  • Choice of handle type – Twisted & Flat Paper Tape, Cotton or P.P Rope, Ribbon and more. Dye to match handles available where possible.
  • Kraft bags are environmentally friendly, recyclable and easily biodegradable.
  • Eco bags are highly durable, re-usable,
    on-trend and are a great way for your brand to be exposed many times over.
