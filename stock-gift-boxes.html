Plain Gift Boxes

We hold 4 sizes of Gift Box in stock; mainly in Plain Black, White and Red.
All of our boxes are Flat Pack style for self assembly and have a separate base & lid.

Our boxes are manufactured to the highest standard and are the perfect compliment to any gift or product.

  • Available in Gloss White, Black and Red.
  • 4 sizes - Accessory, Small, Medium & Large.
  • Please note our Red boxes are only available in the Small and Medium sizes.
  • Flat Pack style for easy storage and self-assembly.
  • No gluing required, our boxes self lock into position.
  • Low quantities; the minimum order quantity is just 10pcs per size/colourway. We also offer further discounts for bulk orders.
  • Next day delivery available (cut-off 3pm). Payment accepted via most major UK debit & credit cards. We also accept PayPal.
  • Available to buy over the phone or via our eBay page.
