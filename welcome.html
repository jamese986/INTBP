Welcome to In The Bag Packaging

Wow; what a busy few months we’ve had here at In The Bag Packaging! We’ve had a bit of a rebrand and a fabulous fresh new website built to showcase just some of our products.

It’s amazing how much time and effort goes into making the above happen but we’ve been working with a fantastic team of people, who put simply, are just brilliant at what they do. James and Steph… you know who you are!

So then, what’s changed you may ask? Well apart from the new website, a trendier new logo and more social media channels, we thought it was time for a refresh. After celebrating more than 10 years in business our company is continuely growing and we are always reaching out to new customers to build on what we do best.

Behind the scenes we are still the same team, but that’s a good thing! With over 20 years experience in print, design and manufacture we are specialists in all types of retail packaging – from Carrier Bags to Gift Boxes and everything in between. We love to see an initial design or idea be turned into a fully functioning piece of packaging. It makes us extremely proud to be able to create such beautiful pieces.

As always, we continue to offer the very best service and most competitive prices to suit your budget and requirement; but never compromising on quality.

Every customer is highly valued to us, no matter how big or small. Some of our customers have been with us since we began trading back in 2003 and have recommended us many times over; this for us speaks volumes. We would therefore like to take the opportunity simply to say - Thank You!

So; if you are new company looking for quality bespoke printed packaging, or if you’re looking for a change in what you have already then we’d love to hear from you.

We’re very social too - you can also connect with us on Facebook, Twitter, Instagram & LinkedIn.

We look forward to hearing from you…

Joanne Smith - M.D.
