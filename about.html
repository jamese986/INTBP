About Us

Packaging is all about first impressions.

With over 20 years experience in packaging design, print and manufacture, we understand the importance of those first impressions. From luxury bags and boxes to everything inbetween, you’ll find our personal touch, attention to detail and customer service is second to none.

As a small independent company, we pride ourselves on giving you more choice and control over your bespoke requirements; right down to the finishing touches that are so important. With our help, your packaging will create the very best first and last impression with your customers.

We are dedicated to offering our timely, trusted expertise and support along your journey, so why not contact us to see what we can do for you. No job is too big, no ask is too big. We think big and we don’t cost a small fortune.
